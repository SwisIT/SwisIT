Halo of Spores
a creature you can see moves into a space within 10 feet of you or starts its turn there
-> reaction 1d4 necrotic damage to that creature unless it succeeds on a Constitution saving throw against your spell save DC. 
-> The necrotic damage increases to 1d6 at 6th level, 1d8 at 10th level, and 1d10 at 14th level.
